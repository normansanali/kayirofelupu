Prince William And Kate Middleton Are Secretly Besties With These Very Famous People

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 29, 2024

The social circles of royals and famous people in different industries are typically distinct and separate. However, a few notable individuals have managed to bridge the gap between these two worlds. Some have formed connections through shared philanthropic interests; others have forged friendships through chance encounters or shared interests.

Famous people like celebrities being close friends with royals are not uncommon in present times. Considered one of the modern royals, Prince William and Kate Middleton’s close friendships outside the royal family have provided them a platform, as they can fulfill their royal duties in a dynamic environment while promoting their causes and raising awareness for significant issues that they are trying to address.

9 Eddie Redmayne Recalled His Time At University With Prince William

Eddie Redmayne and Prince William attended Eton College at the same time. They were both members of The Eton Society and played rugby together for their school team. The Fantastic Beasts actor shared that he often felt sorry for Prince William during their rugby games because everyone wanted to tackle the prince to be able to boast that they tackled a royal.

Despite pursuing different interests in different colleges after Eton, Redmayne still remembers the prince fondly. While Prince William was the target of rugby attacks, he was popular among his peers at Eton and appeared to fit in well.

8 Ian Thorpe Supports Charitable Activities With Prince William

In 2010, Prince William met Australian Olympic swimmer Ian Thorpe during his visit to Australia. Following their meeting, the Prince became a supporter of Thorpe's charitable organization, Ian Thorpe's Fountain for Youth.

This non-profit organization focuses on enhancing health and education opportunities for children. Ian Thorpe, who has considered the royal family among his closest friends since the 2000 Olympics, was a guest at Prince William and Kate Middleton's wedding in 2011.

7 Guy Ritchie Is A Relative And Friend To The Royal Family

Guy Ritchie is an accomplished filmmaker, known for directing popular movies such as Hercules, Lock Stock and Two Smoking Barrels, Snatch, and Revolver. In addition to his successful career in the film industry, Ritchie is also a friend of Prince William and the Princess of Wales, Kate Middleton. He was one of the guests at their royal wedding in 2011 and notably, the sixth cousin of the Princess of Wales.

6 Reese Witherspoon And Kate Middleton Remain Close Friends

Kate Middleton met Reese Witherspoon during her royal tour of Canada and North America back in 2011. In her book Whiskey in a Teacup, Witherspoon recounted her meeting with Kate Middleton. She admitted that she had previously felt "immune" to the obsession with the British royal family until she received an invitation to meet the Princess of Wales.

Recounting her excitement upon receiving the invitation, Witherspoon wrote, "The scream that issued from my lips upon receiving it --you would have thought I was going to die." She went on to describe Middleton as "lovely and warm, elegant and composed," and noted that the princess even told a joke that left her under her spell.

According to Witherspoon, Middleton is not only magnificent, but also a compassionate, socially conscious, and deeply caring person.

Witherspoon recognized the dedication it takes to commit to a life under public scrutiny and in service to others, stating, "I am so in awe of that kind of dedication." The two became close after their first meeting and the Princess of Wales is thought to have sent Reese a gift when her son, Tennessee, was born a year later.

5 Giovanna Fletcher Is Friends With Kate Middleton

Giovanna Fletcher, the wife of McFly singer Tom Fletcher, has become a recent addition to Kate's inner circle of friends. The two first hit it off during Kate's first-ever podcast interview in 2020, where they shared their experiences of motherhood.

Initially, the podcast was expected to last for 45 minutes, but it was extended due to the natural flow of conversation between the two women. Since then, the two have stayed in touch, and Giovanna has spoken highly of Kate's skills as a mother in various interviews. Giovanna was also present at Kate's Shaping Us event, where she walked alongside her.

4 Zara McDermott And Kate Middleton Also Became Close Friends

The former Love Island contestant, Zara McDermott, supported Queen Consort Camilla at a gala event in November 2022, which aimed to combat violence against women and girls. She made another public appearance alongside the royal couple at the launch of Kate's Shaping Us campaign at BAFTA London in December 2022.

Zara revealed on Instagram that she is collaborating with Kate on the new campaign. In her post, Zara expressed her excitement to be working with Kate and discussed how the campaign aims to link early childhood experiences with reducing issues in adulthood. Zara also mentioned that she is continuing to work with Kate on the campaign long-term to ensure it can make real change.

3 Ellie Goulding Is Close To Both Prince William and Kate Middleton

Prince William and Kate Middleton recently revisited a cherished aspect of their wedding day during William's Earthshot Prize Awards ceremony in Boston on December 2, 2022. English singer-songwriter Ellie Goulding, who performed at their 2011 wedding reception, gave a special performance at the event. After the show, the royal couple reportedly caught up with Goulding backstage.

At their wedding reception in 2011, Goulding sang a rendition of ‘Your Song’, originally written by Elton John, as William and Kate shared their first dance. In a 2016 interview with Australian Vogue, Goulding recalled feeling nervous during the performance, noting that her hands were shaking. Despite the nerves, the experience remains a cherished memory for the singer-songwriter.

2 The Beckhams Have A Long-Term Friendship With The British Royals

David and Victoria Beckham have a well-known friendship with members of the British royal family. David Beckham and Prince William first became close while working on London's bid for the 2012 Olympics.

They have since collaborated on other campaigns, such as the anti-poaching initiative #WhoseSideAreYouOn. According to David, he and the princes have a casual and friendly relationship. He also noted that the royals are easy to get along with, and they share a love for sports. It is rumored that their wives, Kate Middleton and Victoria Beckham, also developed a close friendship, bonding over their shared interest in fashion.
