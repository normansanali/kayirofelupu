Sam Asghari is focusing on his acting career as he navigates his divorce from Britney Spears.

Britney Has Been Partying With Mystery Men

While Sam has been focusing on his career, Britney has been partying – and sharing about it on social media. Last week, the singer made headlines for hosting a “divorce party” in which she partied with a number of scantily clad men.

In one particular video, Britney was captured rolling around on a bed without a top, surrounded by sheets. At one point, she lifted her leg into the air, revealing her underwear and thigh-high boots. Another video featured the ex-Disney celebrity with a mysterious man licking her leg. A voice from behind the camera could be heard instructing, "Begin at the boot, buddy."

Additional footage showcased Britney donning a green mini dress, being lifted by a group of bare-chested men close to her outdoor pool.

It's also been reported that Britney has formed an unlikely bond with one of her ex-employees, who has a criminal history.

Britney Allegedly Cheated On Sam With An Employee

Britney’s most recent antics come after it was reported that Sam filed for divorce after he came to believe Britney was cheating on him with an employee.

A source has informed the outlet that there exists a video capturing Britney and the employee in what's described as a "compromising position."

For now, Britney hasn’t directly commented on the allegations.
