Gangsta Boo parents: Who are her father and mother?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024

American rapper Lola Chantrelle Mitchell, known by her stage moniker Gangsta Boo, has passed away. The Three 6 Mafia ex-member and rapper was 43 years old.

According to Fox 13 Memphis, the Memphis-born star was discovered dead on Sunday, January 1 at around 4 p.m. (local time), and no cause of death has been established.

Following the news of Gangsta Boo’s passing, DJ Paul, a member of the Three 6 Mafia, posted a picture of her without a caption.

1993 saw the inclusion of Gangsta Boo, the second female member of Three 6 Mafia after K-9, on the group’s debut album “Mystic Stylez.” “Chapter 1: The End” from 1996 and “Chapter 2: World Domination” from 1997 were her last albums with the group.

Gangsta Boo has worked with a variety of musicians during her career, including Drumma Boy, OutKast, Foxy Brown, T.I., Gucci Mane, Eminem, and Yelawolf.

2020 saw her most recent collaborations with Run the Jewels on “Walking in the Snow” and Junglepussy on “Stamina.”

Gangsta Boo’s parents: Who are her father and mother?

The identities of her parents were not revealed. She had been private about her family life.
