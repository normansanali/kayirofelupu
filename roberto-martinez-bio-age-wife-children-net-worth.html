Roberto Martinez Bio, Career, Achievements, Age, Wife, Children, Parents, Net Worth: – Roberto Martinez is a Spanish football coach and former professional player.

Roberto Martínez Montoliu was born on July 13, 1973. He’s currently the manager of the Belgium national team. Roberto Martinez signed as coach of the Belgium national team on Wednesday, August 3, 2016.

Roberto Martinez Biography

Roberto Martinez was born in Balaguer, Lleida, Catalonia to Roberto Martinez Senior and Amor Montoliu. He started his career with CF Balaguer in the Tercera División.

At age 9, he played his first competitive game for the club’s youth team. Roberto Martinez played at every level of youth football for the club before moving to Real Zaragoza at the age of 16 with whom he won the Copa del Rey.

Roberto Martinez Career

Roberto Martinez started his career with CF Balaguer in the Tercera División. At age 9, he played his first competitive game for the club’s youth team and also at every level of youth football for the club before moving to Real Zaragoza at the age of 16 with whom he won the Copa del Rey.

Martínez served as a studio analyst for ESPN’s coverage of the FIFA World Cup (2010 and 2014 editions), the UEFA European Championship (2012 and 2016 editions), and the 2013 FIFA Confederations Cup.

Roberto Martinez has also been a regular guest on Sky Sports’ Spanish football program Revista de la Liga. In addition, he has made several appearances on the BBC’s Match of the Day. In July 2020, Martinez was unveiled as one of the pundits for CBS Sports Network’s UEFA Champions League coverage.
