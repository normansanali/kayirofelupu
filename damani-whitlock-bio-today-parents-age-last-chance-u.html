Get To Know Damani Whitlock Today, Parents, Age, Last Chance U

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Damani Whitlock loves dogs and likes to play video games. But more than that and everything he loves basketball. Yes, it is one of the athletes of Netflix’s Last Chance U: Basketball Season 2 we speak of.

In the 13 December 2022-released second installment of the series, a whole new roster of players was introduced to viewers for the 2021-2022 session. One of them was Damani. So, let us tell you who he is and what he has been up to since Last Chance U, in this writing called ‘Damani Whitlock Bio’.

Damani Whitlock On Netflix’s Last Chance U: Basketball Season 2

Damani Whitlock’s inclusion in the team seemed somewhat unsafe at the start. But, he made a comeback which left the viewers in awe.

This is how Damani’s story plays out in the series. This documentary goes on to tell the success story of St. Anthony High School and Scale Prep Academy former alum in this way. A multi-sports fanatic Damani becomes a student at the University of Hawaii at Hilo (UHH), an NCAA Division II institute in 2018. During the 2018-2019 session, he gets to partake in four games as a freshman. But, right then, an injury stops him.

After recovery, he was able to get his freshman year back. And so he was back in the field. He dominated the 2019-2020 season and was part of every one of the 26 games that the Vulcans played. At UHH, Damani was at the same time pursuing a degree in Mathematics. Then, when the Covid-19 global pandemic happened, he had to shift back home to help his father.

Later, during the 2021-2022 session, as Damani gets ready to show his skills at ELAC Huskies, just after his recruitment, he becomes ill feeling uncomfortable in the area near his heart. Fortunately, his heart gets diagnosed as structurally and functionally fine, so Damani returns again to put the ball in the basket. He also scores well.

To the cameras, Brandon also detailed his plans for the future. But, since the cameras are now turned away, let’s look at where the athlete is at today.

Where Is Damani Whitlock Today?

Following his time at ELAC, Damani Whitlock started studying at Texas A&M University–Texarkana. This institute is a part of the NCAA Division II circuit. So, while pursuing a degree in Civil Engineering, Damani has also been able to resume his career in the field.

Until December 2022, Damani was therefore learning the guidance of his new Head Coach Ryan Wall. At this time around, he held the position of point guard for the Texarkana Eagles.

So, in this way, Damani was doing well on both fronts, academic included. Damani had always dreamt of becoming either an engineer or a doctor.

Damani Whitlock Age

Because Damani Whitlock was born in 1999, he reached the age of 23 in 2022.

Who Are Damani Whitlock’s Parents?

Damani’s father is Major Overton Whitlock who back in the day studied Physical education at Azusa Pacific University School of Education and Political science at the Dominican University of California. according to his Twitter @MOWhitlockJr, he has had a career as an adjunct professor and a coach. He turned 51 years old in November 2022.

As for Damani’s mother, Malika Davis Whitlock, she studied at Dominguez High and then at Uc Santa Cruz (Biology). She turned 48 in July 2022.

As for siblings, Damani has two sisters, Amira Brianna Whitlock and Ebony Monet Whitlock. Sister Amira went to Eleanor Roosevelt High in Corona, California and she turned 26 in January 2022. Back in the day, on 19 September 2010, Amira took to her Facebook to wish her baby sister Ebony her 7th birthday.

Damani Whitlock Girlfriend

Damani Whitlock cared enough to even mention his liking for soul food, seafood, and Thai on his official athlete BIO. Here, he also revealed that he enjoys sleeping. But his dating life is one thing he did not think of sharing. The LA Dodgers and Oakland Raiders fancier also did not publicize his love life on social media.

Damani Whitlock Height

Until December 2022, Damani Whitlock was standing at 6 feet in height and weighed around 175 lbs. Between his freshman year and now, he lost around 15 pounds.
