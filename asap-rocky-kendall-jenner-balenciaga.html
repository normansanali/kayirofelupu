Kendall Jenner ASAP Rocky Shopping Date

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Kendall Jenner's speculated relationship with ASAP Rocky only continues to heat up. The duo was spotted shopping at Balenciaga in Soho yesterday, just two weeks after getting publicly cozy at Coachella.

Splash News

It was a fitting outing for the fashion-forward pair. Jenner donned a cream knit sweater over a top with massive sleeves, hoop earrings and high-waisted trousers. Meanwhile her beau went for the cool-meets-casual route with an embellished denim jacket, graphic tee and beanie.

Splash News

Despite the heat Jenner has received about the failed Fyre Festival and Pepsi commercial controversies, the 21-year-old model was all smiles during her shopping spree with the 28-year-old rapper.

Splash News

Jenner proves that a little little retail therapy can go a long way, and ASAP is the perfect sidekick, whether it be for new jewelry or roaming the shops in Paris.
