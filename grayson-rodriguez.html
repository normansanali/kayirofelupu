Grayson Rodriguez

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 2, 2024

Grayson Rodriguez is an American professional baseball pitcher in the Baltimore Orioles organization.

He is an alum of Central Heights High School in Nacogdoches, Texas. During his junior years at Central Heights, he went 14-1 with a 0.38 ERA, leading Central Heights to the 3A state title. Later, he signed to play college baseball at Texas A&M University.

Rodriguez’s Draft And Net Worth

In the first round, Rodriguez was drafted by the Baltimore Orioles with the 11th overall selection of the 2018 MLB draft. On June 12, 2018, Rodriguez signed with the Orioles and made his professional debut with the GCL Orioles. He played the whole season with the Orioles, going 0-2 with a 1.40 ERA in nine games (eight starts).

There are no actual details of his net worth, but concerning his $4.3 million contracts with the Orioles, he is entitled to a considerable amount of earnings.
