Jana Kramer Reveals She Sold Her Wedding Ring Amid Divorce from Mike Caussin

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Jana Kramer is moving on.

The 37-year-old actress and country singer announced that she was splitting from her ex husband Mike Caussin in April after six years of marriage.

Jana responded to fan questions on the latest episode of her podcast, Whine Down, and shared that she had made a major decision in the wake of her divorce.

Click inside to read more…

She revealed that she had started renovations on her home and that she was paying for them with money she made from selling her wedding ring.

“I have redone pretty much my entire house…I sold my ring, and that money from the ring paid for the furniture and the new additions to the house,” she said.

“I debated. I’m like, ‘I’m going to sell this ring and then maybe buy myself something.’ Like a bag, or my divorce present. [But] I was like, ‘Honestly, what would make me happiest is to have new energy in the house.’ So I took that money and gave it to [interior designers],” she continued.

She added that she felt it was important for her to redesign her home to make it feel more like her own after the divorce.

“This was our house and we made memories here,” she said. “I needed to create a new space that felt like me and felt like mine. So now, I have someone over right now painting the bar room. I’m going to make it this really cool, girlie wine bar. So that’s helped me a lot.”

Jana also said this is the second ring from Mike she’s gotten rid of, the first being from their previous split in 2016.

“Cause the first ring I was like, ‘I will never put this back on my hand,’” she said. “He had re-proposed to me in Napa a few years ago with this new ring. I took every diamond he ever bought me and I sold them, because I didn’t want to look at them ever again.”
