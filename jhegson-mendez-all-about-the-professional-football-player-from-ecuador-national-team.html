Personal Life

Jhegson was born in North America to a North American mother and father. Jhegson’s mother is a strong supporter of his career endeavors. When we look at Jhegson’s Instagram, we notice him wishing his mother a happy Mother’s Day. He wrote that his mother is the most beautiful thing in the world and that the sacrifice she made for him cannot be expressed with the phrase “thank you.” Even on his birthday, Jhegson praised God for the finest present he could have received: the fact that he was still living with his parents at the age of 25.

Jhegson’s siblings are being investigated. Instead, he has a childhood buddy whom he refers to as his brother in an Instagram post. Anddy Steve is the name of his boyhood pal. They were recently spotted enjoying the summer together in Atacames, Esmeraldas, Ecuador.

Jhegson Mendez’s wife

Jhegson has yet to marry. Jhegson is more interested in having expensive automobiles than in any woman. He has a land cruiser and a Nissan automobile, both of which he purchased in 2020 and 2019.

Jhegson’s participation in the FIFA World Cup 2022

Jhegson’s football career began with the FIFA 2022 World Cup. He joined the Ecuador national squad in 2018 after the country was not qualified for the FIFA World Cup. They qualified in 2022 by finishing as CONMEBOL’s third runner-up on March 24, 2022. Jhegson was named to the Ecuador national team’s 26-man squad for the FIFA World Cup 2022 by their coach, Gustavo Alfaro, and will make his debut in the tournament. On November 20, 2022, he appeared as a midfielder in Ecuador’s opening match against Qatar. Currently, he will play in Ecuador’s second group-stage match against the Netherlands on November 25, 2022.

His new experience and goals for the FIFA World Cup 2022

In an interview before to playing against the Netherlands national team, Jhegson said that competing in the World Cup was a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for Ecuador, as it became a source of pride for the country. He said that his team’s passing confidence rose as a result of their first-match performance. Even though his opponents were already world cup participants, his squad was preparing for future matches. He said that though the other side has extremely talented players, his squad knows how to react to such a tough circumstance, as the team has prepared effectively for counterattacks against midfielders.
