Robert Charles Joseph Edward Sabatini Guccione was born on December 17, 1930, in Brooklyn, New York City, USA, to parents of Italian (Sicilian) ancestry. He was the son of Robert Charles Joseph Edward Sabatini Guccione and Elizabeth Sabatini Guccione. In addition to being a successful businessman and magazine publisher, Bob is most known for being the creator of the adult magazine “Penthouse.” The magazine was intended to be a rival to “Playboy,” and all of his efforts contributed to increasing his net worth to the level it was at the time of his death in 2010. What was Bob Guccione’s net worth? As of early 2017, reports estimated that he had a net worth of $400 million, which he derived mostly from the popularity of the film “Penthouse.” He was included in the Forbes 400 list of wealthiest people in the world in 1982. However, when internet porn grew in popularity, his market shrank, ultimately leading to the collapse of his publishers. Despite this, all of his accomplishments contributed to maintaining his position of riches.
