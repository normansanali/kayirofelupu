Who Is Diane Ladds Spouse?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 2, 2024

Diane Ladd is a famous American actress, director, author and film producer. Over the course of her lengthy acting career, which has spanned more than six decades, Diane has been in more than 200 films and TV shows. She has received praise from critics and won more than 57 international awards, including the “BAFTA,” the “Golden Globe,” and the “Independent Spirit Award.”

Diane Ladd was born in Meridian, Mississippi, United States of America on 29th November, 1935. According to her date of birth, she is 87 years of age as of 2022. Her father’s name is Preston Paul Ladner and her mother also bears the name Mary Bernadette.

She was the only child of her parents. Her father was a travelling salesman who created and distributed his own chicken medicines across the South, while her mother was a housewife and an actress.

She was related to American dramatist Tennessee Williams. She is also connected to Sidney Lanier, an American poet, author, and musician who lived in the 19th century.

Diane Ladd Spouse

From 1960 to 1969, Diane was officially married to an American actor called Bruce Dern. Their first child, Diane Elizabeth Dern, who was born on November 27, 1960, passed away from a catastrophic brain haemorrhage on 18th May, 1962, when she was just 18 months old after falling into a swimming pool. Actress Laura Dern, their second child, was born on 10th February, 1967.

Diane was again, married to William A. Shea, Jr. from April 1973 to 1976. She has been married to Robert Charles Hunter since 14th February, 1999.

On 1st November, 2010, Diane, Laura, and Bruce each received a “star” on the “Hollywood Walk of Fame,” with Diane receiving the 2,421st star. They were the first family to ever get such an honour on the “Walk” thanks to this acknowledgement.
