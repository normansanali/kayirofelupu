What is Susanpod.com?

Susanpod has been categorized as a suspicious site because of the following drawbacks

It has given its parent company’s name as TOP LIFESTYLE LIMITED. However, it could change its address and name in the future due to similar sites.

Susanpod’s website has many details, including a website theme that matches multiple problematic sites.

Our Final Verdict:

These facts lead us to conclude that Susanpod is a suspicious website.
