Triple H Was Very Supportive Of Candice LeRae &amp; Johnny Gargano Having A Baby

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 22, 2024

The world of professional wrestling is unpredictable. Storylines are often altered at the last minute due to unexpected injuries and pregnancies. This is why Candace LeRae wanted to inform WWE officials of her plans to get pregnant ahead of time so that they weren’t put in an awkward position.

Speaking on Out of Character podcast, Candace LeRae talked out the birth of her baby. She mentioned having a meeting with Triple H and Shawn Michaels before revealing her plans to get pregnant. Candace LeRae also discussed how Triple H reacted to the news.

“So earlier in the year, Johnny and I had sat down with DX of all people and discussed the fact that we wanted to have a baby and trying to kind of figure out when the right time was. Of course, they’re like, very supportive. Obviously, we don’t have to let them know but we wanted to kind of keep them in the loop because I felt like I owed it to everybody.

I didn’t want to be put in a position or them be put in a position where we’re all just kind of like, ‘Oh, whoops, now what do we do?’ They were so [supportive]. Hunter even said to me. He said, ‘There’s never gonna be a right time. So you guys got to do what’s best for you guys. And we’ll work around it,’ Essentially.”

Candace LeRae and Johnny Gargano returned recently to Monday Night RAW under the new regime. It looks like WWE may have some big plans for the two of them heading into the future. Only time will tell what those plans are.
