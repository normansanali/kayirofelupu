Brent Faiyaz is an American music producer and artist born on September 19, 1995. He is a member of the group Sonder, who embarked on their first U.S. tour in 2017. Faiyaz has released several solo projects, including his debut album Sonder Son in 2017. He has also collaborated with other artists, including GoldLink and Jorja Smith.
