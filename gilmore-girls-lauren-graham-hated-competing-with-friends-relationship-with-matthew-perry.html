Gilmore Girls Star Lauren Graham Hated Competing With Friends Despite Her Relationship With Matthew

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 27, 2024

Highlights

  • Lauren Graham and Matthew Perry were close friends despite rumors of a romantic relationship.
  • Graham humorously acknowledged the competition of Gilmore Girls airing opposite Friends.
  • Perry cherished their friendship and had a positive impact on Graham.

Lauren Graham and Matthew Perry maintained a close friendship despite numerous rumors of a romantic relationship between them. As Perry's character in Friendschanged due to his battle with substance abuse, Graham also faced challenges with the end of her long-term relationship with Peter Krause.

Despite having worked with Perry before Gilmore Girls, Graham admitted to having reservations about competing with close friends in the unforgiving landscape of showbiz—particularly during a time when each season of Gilmore Girls almost didn't get picked up.

What Lauren Graham Said About Gilmore Girls Airing Opposite Friends

During Lauren Graham's appearance on Late Night with Conan O'Brien, their conversation began with their authentic Irish identity, with Graham jokingly referring to them as the "forgotten people" of Ireland. The banter centered on the stereotypical image of red hair and freckles, a characteristic often associated with Irish heritage.

O'Brien then shifted the focus to Graham's new show during that time, Gilmore Girls. "I'm on at eight opposite Friends. So, together we'll make sweet, sweet music." Graham shared.

"The critics really like this show… They say it's smart, it's funny. And then they put it opposite Friends. How does that feel?" Which, the actress humorously answered:

"That piece of crap, it's going down… Yeah. It's a, you know, it's bad, but someday things will be better. All you can do is control what you can control, right?"

Did Lauren Graham Date Matthew Perry?

Lauren Graham and Matthew Perry were reported to have been romantically linked in 2003, but they never officially confirmed their relationship during that period.

Ellen DeGeneres used to playfully tease Graham about her rumored relationship with Perry whenever Graham appeared on her talk show. Despite the banter, Graham consistently downplayed any romantic involvement. On one occasion, she responded to DeGeneres, "You always make fun of me for not going out with him, and I keep telling you he's just my friend."

In a Q&A during that time, Perry spoke fondly of Graham, labeling her as one of his favorite people, "We have great chemistry when working together and it’s fun to work with a close friend." Perry stated as reported by Deadline.

Inside Lauren Graham And Matthew Perry's Friendship

In the wake of Matthew Perry's unexpected passing, Lauren Graham shared insights into their close friendship. Speaking on CBS Mornings, Graham expressed her shock at the tragic loss and reflected on Perry's enduring legacy.

Graham also emphasized the profound impact Perry had on her and Perry's joy over the positive reception of his book:

"No one made me laugh as hard. I mean, just tears, streaming. There was just such joy in being around him and being his friend… This last year, he was so proud of the book he wrote, and of how many people it touched. It was a success that gave him a level of happiness that I hadn't seen in him for a really long time, so that's a nice memory."

Graham also shared her inclusion in Perry's 2022 memoir, where he posted a photo of them together with the caption, "Me and the beautiful Lauren Graham."
