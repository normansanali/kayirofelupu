Meet Love Island Mady McLanahan! Her Bio, Parents, Height, Age

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 16, 2024

Mady McLanahan was one of two bombshells who entered the villa on Love Island USA season 4 for the first recoupling of the season. And for her secret date, she picked Andy Voyen!

Let’s get to know this radiant lady better as this Mady McLanahan Bio proceeds.

Mady McLanahan On Love Island

Love Island USA returned for its fourth installment on July 19, 2022, and on the very first ep, two bombshells, Mady McLanahan and Valerie Bragg were introduced to the other islanders.

So, up for their task, Mady chose Andy Voyen while Valerie stole Felipe Gomes for a secret date after the first coupling. And their decision was wise! “Mady is my type,” said Andy, not soon after he laid eyes on her.

Later, Mady even took Andy to the treehouse and shared that she wanted to be pursued, was family oriented and that manners were important to her.

Smitten, Andy didn’t want the night or their conversation to end.

Likewise, Valerie also chatted up Felipe, sharing she was a nutritionist, bartender, and college student. Their talk even turned to astrology with Felip and Mady both Gemini, Jesse a Taurus, and Valerie a Capricorn.

Also, Valerie and Felipe shared a long kiss during a game of “Ship Happens.”

On the other hand, Mady just received a hug from Andy, leaving her surprised.

However, Mady and Valerie didn’t make any big decisions on the first night, instead decided to spend some time getting to know the guys.

So, what qualities was Mady looking for in her boyfriend? Well, she wanted someone humble. That’s it! And the biggest red flag would be when he’s too cocky or talks too much about money or possessions

Her last serious relationship was about 6 years before she joined Love Island.

The initial cast this season included Andy Voyen, Courtney Boerner, Felipe Gomes, Deborah Chubb, Isaiah Campbell, Zeta Morrison, Jesse Bray, Sydney Paight, and Timmy Pandolfi. All in a quest for love while also competing in “naughtier and steamier challenges,” as they decided if they wanted to stay with their current partner or “recouple” with someone new.

Mady McLanahan Age

Mady McLanahan was 26 years of age when she appeared on Love Island USA in 2022.

She is of the same age as her co-star Deborah “Deb” Chubb.

Did you know: Mady’s full name is Madison J McLanahan.

Who Are Mady McLanahan Parents?

Mady McLanahan was born to parents, Holly and Denny McLanahan. They were both supportive of Mady’s adventure on Love Island.

Her father, Denny even took it to his FB to write, “It’s official. I can finally post about Mady’s latest adventure. Super proud of her for stepping out of her comfort zone. Prayers for this momma’s heart as we haven’t been able to see or talk to her in a long time…and for her to remember to be a good girl.”

In 2022, Holly and Denny were 49 and 53 years of age respectively.

Sadly, we don’t know much about them. But we do know that Mady looks up to her mother the most in this world. “I look up to you in so many ways and strive to be like you every day,” she dedicated a FB post to her mother.

Other members of Mady’s family include her grandmother named Lacreta McLanahan (81 in 2022), a retired CISD Aide, and an aunt named Mindy McLanahan (62 in 2022), a Pastor at First United Methodist Church of Spearman, TX.

Mady McLanahan Job

Mady McLanahan is a Social Media Marketing Manager who worked at The Vintage Leopard (owned by her parents). Her job there was to handle the Instagram page of the women’s clothing brand. And also, in addition, she helped advertise the brand on her social sites.

The McLanahan family business, The Vintage Leopard, was “committed to providing the best customer service, fast shipping and with the hopes to help empower women and make them feel beautiful.” Overall, it was built on working endless hours to find the newest items that can be offered at reasonable prices.

The store sold trendy apparel, custom jewelry pieces, shoes, totes, accessories, and plenty of gift items.

Here are the store’s IG @vintageleopard and FB @vintageleopard

Besides the family business, Mady also earned commissions featuring items on her Amazon account @madymclanahan.
