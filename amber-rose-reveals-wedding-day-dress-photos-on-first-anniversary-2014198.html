Amber Rose Reveals Wedding Day Dress, Photos on First Anniversary

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Time flies! Amber Rose finally revealed pictures from her wedding day to Wiz Khalifa in honor of the couple’s first anniversary via Instagram on Monday, Aug. 18.

In one photo, the model, 30, looked stunning in a strapless Pnina Tornai for Kleinfeld lace gown with a voluminous skirt of tiered lace and ruffles while holding their then nearly 5-month-old son Sebastian. She wore diamond stud earrings and accessorized with a white headpiece. Khalifa, 26, wore a fuchsia tux, black pants and a matching bow tie.

“Today was the day we said our vows before God and our Family,” Rose captioned the family photo. “I knew as soon as I met u, u were the one for me sweetheart. Happy 1 year anniversary @mistercap and many more to come.”
