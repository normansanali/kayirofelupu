Leah Marlene American Idol Journey

Leah Marlene, when stepped on that American Idol stage for her audition, appeared as someone filled with enthusiasm, and Judge Katy Perry complemented her that she was “not normal”. She had judges with the first song she sang at the audition, Steal My Girl by One Direction.

She then sings an original “Wisher to the Well”. After the song, Katy compared her to Catie Turner. She won the “Yes!” vote from all three judges and proceeded to the Hollywood round.

In the Hollywood Week, she was introduced to the “Indie-Folk” category of the Genre Challenge, in a group with Fritz Hager. She sang “Sk8er Boi” by Avril Lavigne. She was later shown in a duet with Fritz called “Team Green”. They sang “Locked Out of Heaven” by Bruno Mars.

For her final solo performance, she sang “She’s a Self Made Man” by Larkin Poe. The judges pushed her to the Top 24.

In the Top 24, Leah sang “Call Me” by Blondie and earned enough votes, and secured her spot in the Top 20. In that round, she sang “Heal” by Tom Odell and received enough votes to place in the Top 14. Furthermore, Leah sang “Happy Together” by The Turtles, and in the Top 11, she performed “Make You Feel My Love” by Bob Dylan chosen by Katy Perry.

Leah Marlene Net Worth

Leah Marlene should have a net worth under $100 thousand.

As she said it best, Leah Marlene grew up in a house filled with music. She was born in Toronto, Canada, and moved to Normal, Illinois. She began singing and writing her own songs at a very young age.

Her father Derry Grehan (a songwriter and lead guitarist with the Canadian band, Honeymoon Suite) was the biggest influence on her path to becoming a musician. While working on his own music, her father is currently helping her to thrive as a musician.

Read all about her parents here.

Since the age of 10, Leah has been performing live all over Illinois, Nashville, and Canada. For most of her high school years, Leah made regular trips from Illinois to Nashville for songwriting, recording, and performing.

She has been releasing original music since she was 13 years old and most recently released “Noise,” the first and only single off her upcoming album.

Leah has her own verified eponymous YouTube channel which was created over 11 years ago. The channel has 3.32k subs. Besides the cover of several artists, she also uploads her original songs on the platform. Additionally, the channel also houses her live performances and solo gigs from the time she was a little girl.

In November 2020, she also released an album titled The Space Between which is available on Amazon and iTunes. Later, In 2022, Marlene also released her second album titled Many Colors. She has 29,124 monthly listeners on her Spotify account.

According to her LinkedIn, Leah intervened at The Castle Theatre in 2018. She also worked as a team member at Fix Juice in 2020.

Leah completed her high school at Normal Community West High School. She graduated Summa Cum Laude and is an active member of the English, Math, Social Studies, French, Music, and National Honor Societies. She attended Belmont University and studied songwriting but as we know from her audition, she dropped out.
