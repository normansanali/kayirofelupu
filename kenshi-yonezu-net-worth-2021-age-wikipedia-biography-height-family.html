Kenshi Yonezu is best known as a music producer. Producer and songwriter who produced Vocaloid music under the name Hachi, and has scored several major hits in Japan, including “Mad Head Love”, “Poppin’ Apathy” and “Flamingo”. His album Bootleg (2017) topped the Japanese albums charts and was certified double platinum. He was born on March 10, 1991 in Japan. He appeared in rapper Daoko’s song “Uchiage Hanabi”. He started composing music during his junior year in high school, when he formed the band Late Rabbit Edda.
