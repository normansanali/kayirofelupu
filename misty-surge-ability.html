Misty Surge (Japanese: ミストメイカー Mist Maker) is an Ability introduced in Generation VII. Prior to Generation VIII, it was the signature Ability of Tapu Fini. In Generation IX, it is the signature Ability of Galarian Weezing.

Effect

In battle

When a Pokémon with Misty Surge enters the battle, it creates the effect of the move Misty Terrain on the battlefield.

Outside of battle

Misty Surge has no effect outside of battle.

Pokémon with Misty Surge
