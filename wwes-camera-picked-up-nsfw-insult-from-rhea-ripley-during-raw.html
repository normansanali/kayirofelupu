WWEs Camera Picked Up NSFW Insult From Rhea Ripley During RAW

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 8, 2024

WWE’s weekly RAW television show is live, but they have a few seconds of delay just in case they need to hit the censor button. It turns out that whoever is responsible for censoring the audio missed one insult this week.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week

The Judgment Day won tag team turmoil match during RAW this week to claim a #1 contender spot against the Usos for their titles. During that match, Rhea Ripley was on hand to assist in any way she could. This included mocking their opponents in whatever way she wanted.

While the Judgment Day wrestle Cedric Alexander and Shelton Benjamin, Rhea Ripley had a little extra taunt in the chamber. The camera clearly picked up her calling to Alexander to say he is a “dickhead.”

This insult was likely not scripted, so it’s unclear exactly how Triple H felt about it. If anything, “dickhead” isn’t the kind of insult that WWE generally scripts for their talent, so this improvised word was likely all Rhea Ripley’s idea.

We’ll have to see if any other mention of this is made in the future. In the meantime, it certainly made for an entertaining clip. You can check out the video below.
