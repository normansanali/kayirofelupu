Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny Have Paris Dinner Date in Matching Looks

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny are so back.

The couple closed Men’s Fashion Week in Paris on Sunday with a lavish celebration of style that saw the supermodel walk the runway at Place Vendôme to the beat of the reggaeton star’s performance. And once the show was over, the couple retreated to a more private destination—namely, Italian restaurant Ferdi, near the Champs Élysées, where they dined together.

Toward the end of the dinner date, friend and fellow supermodel Gigi Hadid met up with the pair, and the trio headed to a private bash at the Perfumery near the Palais-Royal.

BACKGRID

Khy Sleek Stretch Twisted Top

For the fun evening out, Jenner looked so chic in a steel-gray stretchy suede top-and-pants set from younger sister Kylie Jenner’s clothing brand, Khy. The top was the slinky Khy signature asymmetric shirt, which has a short sleeve on its right side and a twisty strap on the left shoulder. The pants looked like an elevated version of flared leggings. The model seemed to wear no accessories other than a black clutch with the monochrome fit, and opted only for rosy makeup and a glossy lip.

Bad Bunny coordinated with his girlfriend in a light gray suit with an oversize blazer. He wore it over a classic black T-shirt and paired it with a pair of the (not yet released) white and black Adidas ballerina sneakers, elongated black shades, and a gold ring.

More on Kendall

Jenner and Bunny’s romantic Paris Fashion Week trip comes weeks after the two were spotted getting cozy at a Met Gala after-party in New York, where Jenner joined the “Tití Me Preguntó” singer at his VIP table.
