Who was Russell Wilson's First Wife?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 8, 2024

Russell Wilson, the NFL quarterback, was previously married to Ashton Meem before his high-profile marriage to pop singer Ciara.

Their marriage lasted from 2012 to 2014, and while the exact reason for their divorce remains unclear, there have been rumors of infidelity that have circulated. 

Ashton Meem's Background

Ashton Meem was born on September 6, 1987, in Richmond, Virginia. She attended St. Catherine’s High School and later pursued a Bachelor’s degree in communications from North Carolina State University, which she completed in 2010.

After her studies, she embarked on a successful career in the advertising industry, working as an advertising operations assistant at American Family Insurance for over 12 years and as an account executive at Morton Consulting LLC for over 11 years.

Marriage to Russell Wilson

Ashton Meem and Russell Wilson tied the knot on January 14, 2012, at a Country Club in Virginia, the same year Wilson was drafted into the NFL. Unfortunately, their marriage came to an end just two years later in April 2014, when they announced their divorce.

Despite Wilson's request for privacy at the time, there were rumors of a possible affair between Meem and Wilson's former teammate, Golden Tate. However, both Tate and Meem denied these allegations. The exact reason for their divorce remains a subject of public speculation.

Life Post-Divorce

Following the divorce, Ashton Meem has maintained a low-profile life. She has focused on her career and is known for being an advertising operations assistant.

Reports suggest that she has a considerable net worth, which was accumulated mainly through her divorce from Russell Wilson.

Meem prefers to keep a more understated presence, and as of now, there are no reports of her being remarried.

While little is publicly known about Ashton Meem, her marriage to Russell Wilson and subsequent divorce have garnered significant public interest.

Despite the challenges she faced during her high-profile marriage and divorce, Meem has maintained a private and low-key life.

As Russell Wilson has moved on and is now happily married to Ciara, Meem continues to focus on her career and personal life away from the public spotlight.

 

The life of Ashton Meem, as the first wife of NFL quarterback Russell Wilson, remains relatively private, with few details available about her personal life post-divorce. Despite the public interest in her marriage to Wilson, Meem has chosen to maintain a low-profile presence while focusing on her career and personal endeavors.
