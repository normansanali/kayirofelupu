Stars as Ray Manchester / Captain Man on Nickelodeon’s ‘Danger Force’ (2020)

Created by Christopher J. Nowak, Danger Force is an American television series that premiered on Nickelodeon on 28th March 2020. The show is a spin-off of Henry Danger that aired on the network for six years.

As per IMDb, the premise reads:

Captain Man has a new crew of superhero sidekicks - Danger Force. Captain Man and Schwoz create a fake school to train the kids to harness their uncontrollable superpowers to fight crime.

Looking for a part-time job, 13-year-old Henry Hart finds himself helping superhero Captain Man as his sidekick, keeping the secret from his family and friends.

Cooper Barnes made his acting debut with the movie Digging with Earnest (2001). In the later years, he had lots of cameos on small screens, including Californication (2007), Sex Ed (2009), and Cold Case (2009).

Barnes’ next notable appearance came in the black comedy/psychological thriller The Perfect Host (2010). The following year, he played the role of Hawkman in the movie A Toast to Green Lantern (2011).
