Kendall Jenner, Hailey Bieber, and Lori Harveys Girls' Night Out

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Friends who dress up together, stay together. Kendall Jenner, Hailey Bieber, and Lori Harvey just proved that maxim with a stylish girls’ night out yesterday.

The trio were seen enjoying dinner at Santa Monica celebrity hot spot Giorgio Baldi, where they all turned heads with their style.

Jenner arrived on the scene in a dark brown monochromatic ensemble, consisting of a completely sheer bodycon blouse with asymmetrical sleeves tucked into a matching leather pencil skirt. She topped off the look with pointed-toe under-the-knee leather boots in black, which coordinated with her large black croc-embossed clutch. Other accessories included a brown belt cinched at her waist and silver stud earrings.

BACKGRID

Bieber opted for a more casual outfit. The Rhode founder wore a light gray ruched set composed of a one-shoulder crop top and a teeny-tiny miniskirt with an asymmetrical drape tie detail. She layered the outfit underneath a black oversize collared leather jacket, and continued the black accents by carrying a black purse and stepping out in strappy black heels.

BACKGRID

As for Harvey’s look, the model looked incredible in a strapless black ankle-length dress by Shani Shemer with torso cutouts down the center of the chest, with the fabric on the bodice held together by three silver rings. Harvey finished off the outfit with strappy black heels, a tiny black purse, a stack of thick black bangles, and glittering stud earrings.

BACKGRID

Bieber and Harvey had a BFF matching moment earlier this month, when the two were spotted out and about in different leather looks. Bieber wore a long, Matrix-esque leather trench coat over a cropped gray T-shirt and baggy navy blue track pants. Meanwhile, Harvey wore a distressed leather moto jacket, a black baby tee, and oversize black cargo pants.
