Alanna Ubach is an American actress, voiceover artist, director, producer, narrator, writer, singer, and theatre artist who is known for having appeared in different roles in various movies such as Maria in Sister Act 2: Back in the Habit, Noreen in The Brady Bunch Movie, Niccolina in Love Is All There Is, Cherry in Pink as the Day She Was Born, Girl Student in Labor in Shriek If You Know What I Did Last Friday The 13th, Serena McGuire in Legally Blonde and Legally Blonde 2: Red, White & Blonde, Isabel Villalobos in Meet the Fockers, Jenny in A Haunted House, Naomi in Waiting… and Still Waiting…, and Karen in To the Bone. The most remarkable thing about Alanna Ubach’s acting has been her ability to get totally absorbed in the performance and making each of her roles a class apart, making her gain much appreciation as a theatre artist as well. However, her talents are not just restricted to acting and she has also flourished greatly as a voice actress and has been cast in a number of movies and shows such as Batman: Gotham Knight, Ozzy & Drix, Gary & Mike, Rango, Coco, Teamo Supremo, Higglytown Heroes, Brandy & Mr. Whiskers, El Tigre: The Adventures of Manny Rivera, Random! Cartoons, The Spectacular Spider-Man, Pound Puppies, Ben 10: Omniverse, Mad, Sheriff Callie’s Wild West, Welcome to the Wayne, Puppy Dog Pals, and Monsters at Work. Other than that, Alanna Ubach has also appeared in shows like Euphoria, Veronica Mars, Snowfall, Girlfriends’ Guide to Divorce, Hand of God, American Horror Story, Things You Shouldn’t Say Past Midnight, See Dad Run, Californication, Hung, Men of a Certain Age, My Manny, Friday Night Lights, Two Guys, a Girl and a Pizza Place, Touched by an Angel, Seduced by Madness: The Diane Borchardt Story, Access Hollywood Live, and Unapologetic with Aisha Tyler. Additionally, she has also tried out writing, directing, and producing while working on some projects like A mi amor mi dulce and Poolboy: Drowning Out the Fury. Moreover, Alanna Ubach has gained a huge social media fan base as she enjoys more than 250k followers on Instagram and more than 150k followers on Twitter.

    As a Singer

    Alanna Ubach has performed many songs in a few movies and shows like La Llorona in Coco, Cookie’s Lullaby, A Puppy Dog Time of Year, A Pup for Every Person (reprise), A Pup for Every Person, No Puppy Dog Time This Year, and Barlow (final reprise) in Pound Puppies, and I’m on Fire and Everyone Wants to Pluck Daisy in 30 Days Until I’m Famous.

    First Film

    Alanna Ubach made her first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of Gloria in the comedy-drama film, Airborne, in 1993. Directed by Rob Bowman, the film starred the likes of Shane McDermott, Seth Green, and Brittney Powell.

    As a voiceover artist, she made her feature film debut by providing vocals to the character of Dander in the animated (anime-influenced) superhero film, Batman: Gotham Knight, in 2008. The lead cast of the film included actors Kevin Conroy, Gary Dourdan, David McCallum, Jim Meskimen, Ana Ortiz, Corey Burton, Rob Paulsen, and Kevin Michael Richardson.

    In 2011, Alanna Ubach made her theatrical film debut as a voice actress by providing vocals to the characters of Boo/Cletus/Fresca/Miss Daisy in the computer-animated western action comedy film, Rango, whose lead cast included Johnny Depp, Isla Fisher, Abigail Breslin, Ned Beatty, Alfred Molina, Bill Nighy, Stephen Root, Harry Dean Stanton, Ray Winstone, and Timothy Olyphant.

    First TV Show

    Alanna Ubach made her first TV show appearance by playing the role of Willie in an episode, titled It’s My Party, of the family comedy-drama sitcom, The Torkelsons, in May 1992 and the show starred the likes of Connie Ray, Olivia Burnette, Lee Norris, Anna Slotky, Aaron Michael Metchik, Rachel Duncan, William Schallert, Brittany Murphy, Jason Marsden, and Perry King.

    As a voice actress, she made her TV show debut by providing vocals to the additional voices in an episode, titled Washington D.C., of the animated comedy show, Gary & Mike, in February 2001.
