How do you store leftover smoothie bowls?

The Best Way to Save Leftover Smoothie
  • Store your smoothie in an airtight container. Rider recommends filling the container to the very top to prevent air from being trapped in the container. (Air will oxidize the nutrients in your smoothie, thus degrading its nutrients.)
  • Seal your container tightly. Store in the refrigerator.

    Do you put acai bowls in the fridge or freezer?

    Sealed in a glass jar an acai bowl (smoothie portion) will last in the fridge for up to 3 days. I definitely don’t recommend storing it will all the toppings as they will become mushy! To freeze, fill a glass jar about half way to the top with acai smoothie mixture, put a lid on and place it into the freezer.
