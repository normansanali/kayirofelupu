TN Teacher Getting Pepper Sprayed By Student Leaves Netizens Outraged The Talks Today

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Recently a video is going viral over the internet about a student who did something unexpected that no one thinks that she will do over something so small. When the public get to know about the video they all started to go over the internet to know more about it. Everyone is searching for viral videos over the internet as the video already have been spread over social media. The public also left comments on the video expressing their thoughts about female students that were present in the video. In this article, we will give all the details about viral videos. Keep reading the article to know more.

TN Teacher Getting Pepper Sprayed By Student

Since Friday, May 5, 2023, a video of a teacher in a high school in Antioch, Tennessee, being pepper sprayed twice by a pupil has gone viral on Reddit. After the teacher took the student’s phone, the latter was pepper-sprayed by the former. The video was submitted by a Reddit user named @Lazy_Mouse3803, who said that the student was “texting and Googling answers for her school work” when the teacher took her phone away. The commenter also mentioned that this was the second occasion a student had physically hurt a teacher. When a different student was discovered cheating two months prior, the same teacher was hit in the face after taking away the student’s phone. The user claimed that at this Antioch high school, instances like these were frequent.

The student in question is an adult, according to the exchange depicted in the post. Indignant internet users said that she “straight up assaulted” the faculty member in response to the news. The treatment of the teachers at Antioch High School appalled internet users as the video gained popularity. Many people discussed ways to support instructors while also limiting the number of students who bring their phones to school. Cybernauts also questioned if the student was allowed to bring pepper spray to class. They said that she would be responsible for the teacher’s repercussions if she used it if she was deemed mature enough to do so.

Despite not being fatal, pepper sprays can be harmful. They are marketed as aerosols that can be used to defend oneself from attackers. They contain an inflammatory compound called capsaicin in the form of oil, claims Medical News Today. The compound capsaicin is what gives chilli peppers their distinctive spiciness. It causes burning, agony, and tearing when it gets into the eyes. It is harmful since it can also result in temporary blindness, wheezing, shortness of breath, fear, skin blisters or burns, dizziness, and loss of consciousness.
