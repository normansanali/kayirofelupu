Angela Simmons Net Worth 2024

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 5, 2024

Introduction

Angela Simmons, a name that resonates with many, is a renowned American reality TV star, entrepreneur, and fashion designer. She is best known for her appearances in the reality TV series “Run’s House” and its spin-off “Daddy’s Girls”. Angela is also recognized for her entrepreneurial ventures, including her fashion line “Pastry Footwear”. As of 2024, Angela Simmons’ net worth is a topic of interest to many, given her diverse income streams and successful career.

Early Life and Education

Angela Simmons was born in Queens, New York, to Joseph Simmons, better known as Rev Run, a founding member of the influential hip-hop group Run-D.M.C., and Valerie Vaughn. She was raised in a family deeply rooted in the music industry, which significantly influenced her career path.

Angela’s Career Beginnings

Angela’s career took off with the reality TV show “Run’s House”, which showcased the life of her family. The show was a hit and brought Angela into the limelight. She later starred in the spin-off “Daddy’s Girls”, alongside her sister Vanessa, which followed their lives in Los Angeles as they ran their business.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

Angela and her sister Vanessa co-founded the sneaker brand “Pastry Footwear” in 2007. The brand was a success, reportedly generating millions in revenue. Angela’s entrepreneurial spirit didn’t stop there. She has launched several other businesses, including a lifestyle website and a faux fur line, Foofi and Bella.

Reality TV Career

Besides her entrepreneurial ventures, Angela’s reality TV career has significantly contributed to her net worth. After “Run’s House” and “Daddy’s Girls”, Angela appeared in other reality TV shows like “Growing Up Hip Hop” and “The Real”.

Angela’s Influence in the Fashion Industry

Angela’s influence extends beyond reality TV and entrepreneurship. She is a fashion icon, often seen at fashion weeks and has even been a guest editor for Teen Vogue. Her fashion line has been well received, further increasing her net worth.

Philanthropy

Angela is also known for her philanthropic efforts. She has been involved in several charitable causes, including the “Text Kills” campaign against distracted driving and PETA’s campaigns.

Personal Life

Angela’s personal life has also been a topic of interest to many. She has a son with her ex-fiancé Sutton Tennyson, who was tragically killed in 2018. Angela has been open about her struggles as a single mother, which has endeared her to many of her fans.

Real Estate Investments

Part of Angela’s wealth is tied up in real estate. She owns a luxurious home in Los Angeles, which has been featured in several episodes of “Growing Up Hip Hop”.

Endorsements and Partnerships

Angela has also earned a significant amount from endorsements and partnerships with various brands. She has partnered with companies like Samsung, Teami Blends, and Indique Hair, among others.

Net Worth Growth

Angela’s net worth has seen a steady increase over the years. In 2020, her net worth was estimated to be around $7.5 million. By 2024, it has grown to an estimated $13 million, thanks to her successful businesses, reality TV career, and endorsements.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Angela Simmons’ net worth in 2024 is a testament to her hard work, entrepreneurial spirit, and talent. From reality TV to fashion design and entrepreneurship, Angela has diversified her income streams, leading to a significant increase in her net worth. Despite facing personal challenges, Angela has remained focused on her career and continues to inspire many with her success.
